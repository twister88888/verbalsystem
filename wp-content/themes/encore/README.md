## Description

Encore takes a simple and organized approach to presenting your content with
understated charm and undeniable appeal. You can display your music and videos
in unique, gridded archives; showcase a playlist in a distinct, site-wide
player; and much more. Deliver what your fans want with Encore.

## About AudioTheme

AudioTheme is a platform for powering music-oriented websites, allowing for easy
management of gigs, discography, videos and more. The built-in features help you
collect your content in one central location that you own and control.

Find out more about the AudioTheme framework at:
https://audiotheme.com/view/audiotheme/
